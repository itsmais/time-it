How It Works
Time It is a tracker for your tasks. Although it seems like it now only offers basic fucnctionality, it takes the credit for being easy to use, cross-platform, and ads and registration-free. Trying Time It in the browser is also an option before deciding to download.
Time It take privacy seriously, so it does not store any information about you. It do not even ask for your email. All your work data (times and fees) will be locally stored on your machine. You can export or delete this data at any point in time. You are the sole owner of your data here.
The source code for both the app and this website is available for everyone on Github (link in footer).
