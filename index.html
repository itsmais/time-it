Why Time It?

How It Works
Time It is a tracker for your tasks. It works whether your employee asked you to keep track of the time spent at work, or if you are careful about time in general. You can try it in the browser without the need to download as a measure to know whether it is the kind of software you are looking for. We take privacy seriously, so Time It does not store any information about you. We do not even ask for your email. All your work data (times and fees) will be locally stored on your machine in a .csv file. You can export this file at any point in time.
